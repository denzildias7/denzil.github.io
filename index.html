Project

Role: Team Leader

Title: Enhancing the production of bio-diesel from fish oil using nanamaterials.

Description: Bio-diesel is one form of renewable energy. This Bio-diesel fuel is manufactured from vegetable oils, animal fats, reusable materials etc. Like petroleum diesel, bio-diesel can be used in compression ignition engines.Due to the increase in price of petroleum products and automobile emissions contributing to environmental pollution, bio-diesel is emerging as a developing area with high concern. We have conducted the experiment for 1 litre of fish oil to which 300ml of methanol and other chemicals were used in the process. After carrying out transesterification, washing, drying and separating of bio-diesel, the yield obtained was Biodiesel=950ml and Glycerol=250ml. After obtaining the pure bio-diesel, it was taken in a bottle and kept in a cool place.Several properties of bio-diesel was determined such as viscosity, density, flash point and fire point.
